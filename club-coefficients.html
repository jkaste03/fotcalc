Club coefficient ranking


This UEFA 5-year ranking decides seeding and pot-distribution in qualifying rounds and the group stage. A higher ranking gives the club weaker opponents in the mentioned phases.

The ranking is based on club coefficient points. See also the 10-year coefficient ranking.
